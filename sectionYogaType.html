Types of Yoga

Acro yoga

It stretches and relaxes your body,
improves stamina and memory.

Vinyasa yoga

It serves as a training to increase
strength and helps to build lean muscle
mass throughout the body.

Hatha yoga

It introduces beginners to the main
relaxation techniques and asanas.

Kundalini yoga

It helps relax and restore the mind and
the body, improving strength, flexibility,
and endurance.
